Liminal catches what you're about to drop.

You run your company across email, Slack, meetings, docs, notes, AI sessions and a calendar. The context that matters — what you promised, what was decided, what changed, what you're waiting on — is scattered across all of them and lives in none of them. Liminal reads those surfaces and keeps one receipted ledger: what you owe, what's owed to you, what changed, and what needs you next — every line checkable to its source. Your data stays on your machine.
