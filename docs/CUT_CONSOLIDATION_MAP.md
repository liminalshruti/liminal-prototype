## §1 · The adjudication: one master, and which file hosts it

The two prior sources agree on substance and disagree on substrate:

- The **fold map** (06-13) says: *"11-govern → becomes the Agency master"* — generalize
  the richest live chrome, add the subject switch, upgrade slate/tray/agents to cut
  01's grammar (fold map §3–4).
- **TAXONOMY** (06-16, newer) says: cut 00 *"is the shell"* — `SUBJECTS={spend,custody,
  osint,notice,pattern}` already drives one render loop; *"the others are panes of
  this."*

**Ruling:** these are the same architecture at two layers, and the consolidation splits
it accordingly:

1. **The mechanism** (cut 00's contribution) moves to `lib/loop.js` +
   `lib/evidence-panes.js` — the subject-parameterized loop engine. Cut 00 is the
   *sketch* of the engine; once the engine exists, 00's inline implementation is
   superseded and the file archives (with a redirect, §4).
2. **The master surface** (fold map's ruling stands) is **cut 11 generalized**: it is
   `live` maturity, carries the Today surface (cut 10 already absorbed per fold map
   §1), owns the wedge→infra seam (TAXONOMY: "positioning's exact worked example"),
   and is what `_demo-lan` presents from. It gains: the subject switch, the five
   evidence panes, the register reskin, and cut 01's orbital + tray→slate grammar
   (fold map §4).
3. **The demonstration strategy survives the fold in substance, not in file.**
   "Cut-01-forward, shell as proof" (TAXONOMY, DECIDED 2026-06-16) becomes: the
   catalog front door is the **master entered at subject=pattern, framed wedge**
   (founder read, orbital + tray grammar preserved) — entered as founder-OS, priced as
   infrastructure, exactly as decided. Cut 01's file becomes a redirect to that
   coordinate (§4), so the strategy's front door never breaks.
